Cultivating coding culture among Bruneians

WHAT IS CODING.BN?

Coding.bn is a 6-month course program initiated by AITI Brunei to train unemployed youths with coding/programming skills aligned with demand in the industry. During these 6 months course and mentorship program, participants will have the opportunity to undertake apprenticeship in companies to work on real projects allowing them to experience a real working environment!
